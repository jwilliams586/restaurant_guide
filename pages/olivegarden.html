Olive Garden

Location


View Larger Map

The Olive Garden is a national chain restaurant and offers many Italian pasta dishes. It is a great place for a sit-down meal, whether it's lunch or dinner. It is a nice option for UM-St. Louis students who want to splurge a little with friends or family. It offers pasta, pizza, and their signature unlimited salad and bread sticks. It's food is both nutritious and satisfying while staying within a person's budget. They also have full bar of drinks and non-alcoholic drinks as well.

Olive Garden's menu offers vegetarian dishes, as well as gluten-free dishes for those with dietary needs. Their menu offers beef, chicken, seafood, and many of their dishes are under $10.00.

Popular Menu Items

Chicken Parmigianino ($9.95)

Parmesan-breaded chicken breast fried and topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese. Served with spaghetti.

Egg Plant Parmigiana ($8.95)

Lightly breaded egg plant; fried and topped with marinara sauce, mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Served with spaghetti.

Fettuccine Alfredo ($12.25)

Parmesan cream sauce with a hint of garlic, served over fettuccini.

Capellini Pomodoro ($10.75)

Roma tomatoes, garlic, fresh basil and extra-virgin olive oil tossed with capellini.
