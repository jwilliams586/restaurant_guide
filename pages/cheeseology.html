Cheese-ology

Location


View Larger Map

Cheese-ology is a restaurant in the Delmar Loop that specializes in macaroni and cheese. Their menu offers over a dozen eccentric varieties of macaroni and cheese. They also have limited edition macaroni and cheese options that change on a regular basis. Previous limited edition options have included Spinach Artichoke Mac, Jambalaya Mac, and Lobster Mac. They also serve a selection of beer on tap, beer in bottles, and wine.

At Cheese-ology, there are options for anyone who likes macaroni and cheese. They have several options for vegetarians, and a Vegan Mac. The Vegan Mac comes with the option of vegetable and spice add-ins. They offer gluten free noodles upon request as well.

Popular Menu Items

The Hill ($7.00)

Provel cheese and Salsiccia (sausage) are balanced by a top layer of Marinara

Philly Cheese ($7.50)

American and Provolone cheese, Steak, Onions, and Peppers.

Mediterranean ($8.00)

Mozzarella and Goat cheese, Artichoke Hearts, Sun Dried Tomatoes and Kalamata Olives. This is a vegetarian macaroni and cheese.

Chicken Cordon Bleu ($6.50)

American and Gruyere cheese, Dijon mustard, Chicken and Canadian Bacon.
