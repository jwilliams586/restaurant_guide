Salvatores Italian Grill

Location


View Larger Map

Salvatores Italian Gill has a lot of options for any Italian lover. Many pastas, sandwiches, pizza, and even burgers. They have some house specialties and even coupons on their website.

With live music on the weekends and many events on other days, there's always something to enjoy at Salvatores Italian Grill.

Popular Menu Items

Pizza ($7.49-$16.85)

Pizza dough mad fresh daily, all pizzas have a choice of Mozzarella, Provel, or a blend of both.

White Lasagna ($11.99)

Fresh spinach and mushrooms with ground beef in a white cream sauce and topped with cheeses. Made to order and baked in our brick oven. Please allow 30 min to prepare.

Deluxe Stromboli ($10.50)

Ham, Salami, pepperoni with a blend of cheeses, green peppers, onions & mushrooms.
