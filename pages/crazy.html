Crazy Bowls & Wraps

Location


View Larger Map

Crazy Bowls and Wraps is a local chain restaurant with many locations throughout the St. Louis area. They Specialize in rice bowls, noodle bowls, salad bowls, and wraps. They offer the convenience and affordability of a fast-food restaurant, but with a better quality and nutritional value of food.

Crazy Bowls and Wraps is a good option for students who want to eat healthy or students with dietary restrictions. Their menu is very customizable: you choose the type of protein, type of grain, and type of sauce used in many of their menu items. They also offer vegetarian and vegan options, which are labeled on their menu.

Popular Menu Items

Stir Fry Veggie Bowl ($4.50/5.50)

Steamed vegetables served with your choice of grain and your choice of sauce (Thai, garlic ginger, olive oil-herb-lime, jalapeno cilantro, or sesame)

Fajita Wrap ($6.95)

Sauteed fajita vegetables in jalapeno-cilantro lime sauce with beans, lettuce, tomato, avocado, sour cream, and cheese on a tomato tortilla. Served with tortilla chips and choice of grain and protein.

Asian Chicken Salad ($6.95)

Grilled chicken breast with fresh salad mix tossed with crisp lo mein noodles and ginger dressing and topped with goma shio.

Sweet and Sour Chicken ($5.95/7.15)

Crunchy chicken, red pepper, and onion sauteed in homemade sauce.
