Rooster

Location


View Larger Map

Rooster is a popular destination for brunch located downtown in the Loft district. They specialize in crepes, but there are many other breakfast and lunch options available as well. They also have a bar that serves coffee and alcoholic beverages. They offer Mimosas and eleven quirky varieties of Bloody Mary.

Rooster is a good choice for someone with dietary restrictions. Their menu is very customizable and they have a gluten-free, vegan crepe available upon request. Also, all of their vegetarian options are clearly labeled in their menu.

Popular Menu Items

Breakfast Burrito ($8.75)

Breakfast crepe filled with egg, fontina cheese, potatoes, and house-made salsa

Three Cheese ($8.25)

Savory crepe filled with emmenthaler cheese, asiago cheese, fontina cheese, oven-dried tomatoes, and fresh basil

Croque Monsieur ($7.95)

Sandwich with thinly-sliced roasted ham, caramelized onions, emmenthaler cheese, and romaine lettuce. It also comes with your choice of mixed greens or breakfast potatoes.

S’more ($6.75)

Sweet Crepe filled with crumbled cookies, marshmallow, and dark chocolate
