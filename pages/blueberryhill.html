Blueberry Hill

Location


View Larger Map

Located in the Delmar Loop, Blueberry Hill has many options on their menu. They have private rooms and offer catering. Live music is included most nights and Chuck Berry often plays at this location. They also have darts, pinball, and video games.

Popular Menu Items

  • Chef Salad ($6.95)
  • Vegan Vegetable Soup ($3.50 $4.75)
  • Veggie Burger ($5.95)
  • Red Beans and Rice ($7.50)
