Marios Donuts & Cafe

Location


View Larger Map

Known for their delicious donuts, Mario's Donuts & Cafe also has great food besides the donuts. Open 7 days a week, you can stop by Marios any day.

They have many specials and coupons, and you will eventually give you the option to order ahead of time online.

Popular Menu Items

Mario's Gyro ($5.99)

Grilled Lamb and Beef combined with Mediterranean spices topped with lettuce, tomatoes, onions and Tzatziki sauce served on a warm pita.

Falafel ($4.99)

Blend of Garbanzo beans, mixed spices, parsley served with tomatoes, lettuce, pickles and hummus in hot pita bread topped with tahini sauce

Philly Cheese Chicken ($5.99)

Our version of the famous Steak made with chicken and grilled onions, mushrooms, and green peppers topped with provolone cheese on hoagie.
