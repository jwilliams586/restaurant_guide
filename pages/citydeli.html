Blues City Deli

Location


View Larger Map

Blues City Deli is a restaurant that specializes in deli sandwiches inspired by the "Blues Highway" cities of St. Louis, New Orleans, Memphis, and Chicago. They also serve a selection of salads and hot dogs. Their menu items are very affordable, with most items costing less than $7. They also serve a selection of approximately 18 different varieties of beer.

Blues City Deli also has a blues theme. They have a variety of blues memorabilia on their walls and there is live blues music at the restaurant on every Thursday and Saturday. A music schedule of bands and performance times is available at the Blues City Deli website.

Popular Menu Items

The Benton Park Po-Boy ($6.25)

Smoked Ham, Top Round Roast Beef, Oven Roasted Turkey, Provel Cheese, Dressed with Lettuce, Tomato, Pickle, Onion, Pepperoncini, topped with Sweet Red Pepper sauce and served on a seeded baguette

BBQ Pulled Pork Po-Boy ($5.95)

Applewood Smoked Pulled Pork, Served with St. Louis (sweet n' smokin') or Tennessee (tangy) BBQ Sauce

The St. Louie Primo ($4.75)

Genoa Salami, Cappicola, Mortadella, Pepperoni, Provolone Cheese, Tomato, and Onion... topped with Our House Vinaigrette Dressing. Served on a Italian Seasoned and Toasted Muffuletta Bun

Panino Fresco ($5.25)

Fresh Mozzarella, Provolone Cheese, Our Homemade Olive Salad, Tomato, Onion, and a dash of Our House Vinaigrette Dressing. Served on a Italian Seasoned and Toasted Muffuletta Bun
