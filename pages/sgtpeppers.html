Sgt. Pepper's Cafe

Location


View Larger Map

Sgt. Pepper's Cafe is a restaurant located in Edwardsville, Illinois and Wood River, Illinois with a diner/cafe atmosphere. They have a large, diverse menu that is seven pages long. Menu options include breakfast specialties, wraps, burgers, and their specialty menu item - The Horseshoe.

Another interesting aspect of Sgt. Pepper's cafe is the Beatles Theme. As one might suspect, their restaurant name is inspired by the Beatles and they have a variety of Beatles memorabilia on the walls.

Popular Menu Items

Sgt. Pepper's All Famous Horseshoe ($6.99 / $8.99)

Choice of steak burger, ham, turkey, roast beef, served on toast and topped with French fries and smothered in your choice of spicy white cheese or famous golden cheese sauce. Other specialty horseshoes are available as well such as grilled chicken and veggie.

Honey Dijon Chicken Sandwich ($6.99)

5 oz. chicken breast with melted Swiss and grilled mushrooms, topped with honey Dijon and served on a Kaiser bun with lettuce and tomato.

Veggie Skillet ($6.99)

Sauteed bell peppers, onions, mushroom, tomato, 2 eggs, and your choice of country fried potatoes or hash browns, and shredded cheese.

Southwestern Mexican Salad ($8.99)

Lettuce, diced tomato, bell pepper, black beans, corn, seasoned grilled chicken, shredded cheese, layer of cool mexi-ranch, and tortilla curls, served with bite-size cheese quesadillas.
