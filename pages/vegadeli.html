Vegadeli

Location


View Larger Map

Vegadeli is the perfect option for students with dietary restrictions. Their menu is 100 percent vegan and they have a large selection of raw foods. They have a variety of gluten free options available, which are marked on their menu. Also, their menu changes daily so they have always have an eclectic selection of foods.

Vegadeli is a good option for anyone who is interested in healthy eating as their menu has an emphasis on fresh fruits and vegetables as well as whole grains.

Popular Menu Items

(Their menu changes daily. These are examples of menu items that have been served in the past and their prices)

Marinated Kale Salad ($8.99)

Kale, red cabbage, red onion, tomato, bell pepper, and carrot in signature sesame oil dressing.

Veggie Stuffed Cornbread ($3.50)

Cornmeal and wheat flour stuffed with corn, parsley, spinach, and carrots

Sesame Lemon Pasta ($7.99)

A light pasta with carrots, lemon, zucchini, and sesame oil

Raw Tacos ($7.49)

A blend of walnuts, carrots and spices served on sprouted warps (sprouted warps are not raw)
