Chipotle

Location


View Larger Map

Chipotle is a modern restaurant that busy UM-St. Louis students can enjoy as well as anyone else. Chipotle has a utilitarian appearance of oak wood and steel furniture, and outdoor seating too. It is simple and attractive, and plays music that is young and hip, held at conversational level.

Chipotle not only has meat dishes, but also offers vegan and vegetarian dishes as well. Their food is hormone free, and organically grown. Beer, drinks and wine are also available as well as soda and bottled drinks, as well as a children's menu.

Popular Menu Items

(All dishes can be modified with chicken, beef, beans, or vegetarian beans.)

Burritos ($6.95)

Tortilla, choice of rice, beans, meat, salsa and cheese or sour cream.

Tacos ($5.95)

Choice of three crispy corn or soft flour tortillas with meat, salsa, cheese or sour cream and romaine lettuce.

Salads ($5.95-6.95)

Chopped romaine lettuce with choice of beans, meat, salsa and cheese, with chipotle-honey vinaigrette.

Vegetarian ($5.95-$6.35)

Comes with freshly made guacamole and vegetarian black beans.
