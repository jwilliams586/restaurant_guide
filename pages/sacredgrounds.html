Sacred Grounds Cafe

Location


View Larger Map

Sacred Grounds Cafe is the only vegetarian restaurant in Edwardsville. They offer wraps, paninis, salads, soup of the day, and other special menu items. They also have a case of house-made baked goods available at the counter. Vegan options are available as well. Sacred Grounds Cafe also has a weekend brunch that is very popular.

Sacred Grounds Cafe is a good location for anyone who is interested in a comfortable place to study or hangout. They offer coffee and tea beverages as well as a wide variety of all natural soda, juices, and energy drinks, most of which can be bought with $2 or less. They also have an assortment of board games and a guitar available for patrons to use.

Sacred Grounds Cafe is located on Main Street in downtown Edwardsville. The inside of the cafe has an ever-changing art wall that features the works of local artists. There is plenty of inside seating as well as a few sidewalk tables that are ideal for experiencing the fun atmosphere of Edwardsville's Main Street on a nice day or night.

Popular Menu Items

Hummus Wrap ($4.00)

hummus and tabouli (with bulgar wheat, cucumber, tomatoes and carrots) wrapped in a whole wheat tortilla

Spinach Apple Salad ($4.00)

fresh spinach, shredded carrot, chopped apples, toasted almonds, sesame vinaigrette

Cyprus Panini ($4.00)

grilled eggplant, spinach, tomato, artichoke hearts, and olive tapenade
