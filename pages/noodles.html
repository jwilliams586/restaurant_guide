Noodles & Co.

Location


View Larger Map

Noodles & Company has good variety for everyone. Some dishes are Asian, some Mediterranean, and some are American. Many locations are in the St. Louis area. Soft-pop music plays, and outdoor seating is available. Noodles & Company is convenient, the staff is eager to please, and their meals are good comfort food, and nutritious.

Noodles & Company offers comfortable dining for anyone, and has carry-out available. Their menu has a mix of ethnic foods, as well as standard American dishes. They offer vegetarian and vegan dishes and they have food substitutions for food allergies. Their prices are under $10.00, and have prices for a small bowl, a regular and a trio. Fountain drinks are available also.

Popular Menu Items

($3.95-$6.95)

Japanese Pan Noodles- (Spicy)

Caramelized udon noodles in a sweet soy sauce with broccoli, carrots and shitake mushrooms. Asian spouts, black sesame and cilantro garnish. Sauteed beef excellent with this.

Bangkok Curry-

Coconut curry wok tossed with broccoli carrots, red pepper, onion, mushrooms and a light portion of rice noodles. Served on cabbage with black sesame on top. Shrimp is recommended with this dish.

Penne Rosa-

Spicy tomato cream sauce sauteed with penne pasta, mushrooms, tomato and spinach. Topped with feta or parmesan and Italian parsley. Parmesan Crusted Chicken is great with this.

Wisconsin Mac & Cheese

Scrumptious blend of cheeses and fresh cream served over tender elbow macaroni. Topped with cheddar jack. Anything is good with this.
