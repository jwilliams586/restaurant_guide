St. Louis Bread Co.

Location


View Larger Map

The St. Louis Bread Company is a chain restaurant that specializes in home crafted bread that is delicious. Plug-in your pc; read their newspapers and enjoy breakfast, souffles, lunch, dinner, or pastries. They also offer various coffees, teas, and fruit smoothies. Any UM-St. Louis student would find a healthy and satisfying meal at the St. Louis Bread Co.

Their menu lists various Panini sandwiches, salads, and daily specials on their soups, desserts, and breakfasts. Cinnamon Raisin French Toast is this month's featured breakfast treat. St. Louis Bread Co. opens daily for early risers and is a great place to grab a hot/cold meal for busy UM-St. Louis students.
