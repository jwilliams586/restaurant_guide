Rigazzis

Location


View Larger Map

Since 1957, Rigazzis has been serving up great Italian food on the Hill for many years. So long, in fact, that they are the oldest restaurant located on the Hill.

Home of the frozen fishbowl, Rigazzis offers many different daily specials. They take this one step further in offering more specials through the Foursquare app.

Popular Menu Items

Rigazzis Baked Ravioli (14.95)

Meat stuffed ravioli baked with cheese in meat sauce

Three Topping Burger (8.95)

100% pure ground beef, includes fries, lettuce, tomatoe, onions, and picles on the side.

Rigazzis Special Deluxe Pizza (13.95)

Sausage, Hamburger, Onion, Mushrooms, Pepperoni
