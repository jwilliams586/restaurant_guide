Adrianas

Location


View Larger Map

A great sandwich cafe located on the Hill, Adrianas is a family owned restaurant with many choices available. Many of the sandwichs are named after family members.

They have pizza, pasta, salads, and sandwiches. Be sure to take advantage of their daily specials.

Popular Menu Items

The Hill Boy ($7.50)

Ham, Salami, Mortadella and Provel Cheeses, Lettuce, Tomato, Onion, Pepperoncini, and our Special Dressing

The Faz ($8.50)

Genoa Salami on Hot Garlic Cheese Bread topped with Black Olives and Onions

Sunny's Veggie ($8.50)

Hot Garlic Bread topped with Mozzarella and Parmesan Cheeses, Lettuce, Tomato, Onion, Green Pepper, Artichoke Hearts, and Pesto Dressing
