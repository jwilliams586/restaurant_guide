Ciceros

Location


View Larger Map

Ciceros is found in University City near many other great locations. They have been around since 1977 and have become a popular restaurant in The Loop. They have a wide selection of food, including italian, burgers, salads, and wraps. Ciceros has 53 different beers on draft and 155 in bottle.

Live entertainment is available seven nights a week at Ciceros. Many local acts often play but they have national acts as well. They have a large-screen TV if you want to catch a sports game, and they even have a game room.
