Central Cafe

Location


View Larger Map

Central Café has a retro diner feel in the Central West End. It offers Middle Eastern food in easy dishes for a quick breakfast, lunch or dinner. The busy UM-St. Louis student can take out a meal, or dine in and enjoy the eclectic decorations.

Central Café not only has Middle Eastern meals, but is also features pastries. Their menu has eleven kinds of baklava, baguettes, and dough shells with filling inside, as well as vegetarian and vegan dishes which occupy half of the menu. Non-alcoholic beer and Sangria are also offered.

Popular Menu Items

Falafel Sandwich ($6.99)

Ground chickpeas mixed with spices and fried crispy and golden brown

Chicken Shawarma ($5.49)

Thinly sliced chicken marinated and cooked on a rotisserie with tomatoes, pickles and garlic sauce.

Fatoush Chicken ($9.99)

Romaine lettuce mixed with crunchy pita chips and vegetables topped with sliced grilled chicken.

Beef Kabobs ($13.99)

Cubed beef skewered with fresh vegetables and grilled.
